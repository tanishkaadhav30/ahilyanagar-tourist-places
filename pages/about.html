About Ahilyanagar

Home About Places

Ahilyanagar (formerly Ahmednagar) is a historical city in Maharashtra. It was founded in 1494 by Ahmad Nizam Shah. The city is known for its rich history, forts, temples and cultural heritage.

Many tourists visit Ahilyanagar every year to see famous places like Ahmednagar Fort, Shani Shingnapur and Meherabad,bhisbag mahal, rehekuri blackbuck sanctuary.
