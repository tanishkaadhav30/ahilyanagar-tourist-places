About Ahilyanagar

Ahilyanagar (formerly Ahmednagar) is a historical city in Maharashtra. It was founded in 1494 by Ahmad Nizam Shah.

The city is known for its rich history, forts, temples and cultural heritage.

Many tourists visit Ahilyanagar every year
